Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our quiz platform

How do I create a quiz? +

Upload a text file containing learning material, and our AI will automatically generate quiz questions for you. Simply click the "Generate Quiz" button after uploading your file.

Can I track my quiz scores? +

Yes, your quiz results are displayed immediately after submission along with correct answers and improvements.

Is the quiz generation free? +

Yes, basic quiz generation is completely free. You can upload text files and generate unlimited quizzes with no cost.

What file formats are supported? +

We support plain text (.txt) files. Ensure the content is clear and well-structured for optimal question generation.

How accurate are the generated questions? +

Our AI analyzes your text and produces questions based on sentence structure and keywords. Better input text results in better questions.

Can I customize the quiz difficulty? +

Yes, you can select easy, medium, or hard difficulty, and the AI will adapt the level of complexity.
