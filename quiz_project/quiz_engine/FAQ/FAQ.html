Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our quiz platform

How do I create a quiz? +

Upload a text file containing learning material, and our AI will automatically generate quiz questions for you. Simply click the "Generate Quiz" button after uploading your file.

Can I track my quiz scores? +

Yes, your quiz results are displayed immediately after submission. You can see your score, correct answers, and areas for improvement.

Is the quiz generation free? +

Yes, basic quiz generation and practice are completely free to use. You can upload text files and generate unlimited quizzes without any cost.

What file formats are supported? +

We currently support plain text files (.txt) for quiz generation. Make sure your text file contains clear, readable content for best results.

How accurate are the generated questions? +

Our AI analyzes your text content to create relevant questions. The accuracy depends on the quality and clarity of your input text. Well-structured content produces better questions.

Can I customize the quiz difficulty? +

Yes, you can choose from easy, medium, or hard difficulty levels when generating your quiz. The AI will adjust question complexity based on your selection.
